about

I am a current MMSc Immunology Student at HMS interested in innate immunology, NK cell biology, and host-pathogen interaction in the context of chronic infection. Previously, I completed my BSc in Biomedical Sciences and Drug Mechanisms at UCL. My BSc dissertation project at the Reeve's Lab was on the induction of adaptive-like and tissue resident traits in circulating NK cells in the context of HCMV infection. Prior to that, I was a research assistant at the Biozone Lab for Metabolic Systems Engineering at the University of Toronto's Department of Chemical Engineering.
